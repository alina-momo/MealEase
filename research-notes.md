# Research Notes Used for the Website

The website structure was inspired by common meal delivery and meal kit patterns:

- Meal delivery sites commonly present flexible weekly menus and plans.
- Meal kit businesses emphasize pre-portioned ingredients delivered to the customer.
- Meal subscription shops often use weekly delivery plans and repeat ordering.
- Delivery checkouts often include instruction fields such as ring bell, leave at door, or call on arrival.

The LeafBite website applies these patterns to the provided business plan: hot meals, subscriptions, ready-to-cook packs, frozen food, hygiene transparency, cash payment, and Chattogram-focused delivery.
